Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT)

Bariks is revolutionizing Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT) with a portable, foldable oxygen pressure chamber, making life-saving treatments accessible worldwide. The company aims to provide HBOT treatments anywhere, with the potential to save lives and significantly enhance quality of life.

By combining portability with advanced functionality, our innovative technology minimizes the size of all components, including a unique software system, resulting in a fully self-contained product. This breakthrough offers unmatched versatility, allowing patients, athletes, and anyone in need to undergo therapy with maximum comfort, wherever they may be.
